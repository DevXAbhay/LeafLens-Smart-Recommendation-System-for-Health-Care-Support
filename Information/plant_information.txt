Common Uses
- Aloe Vera gel is commonly used in skincare products, lotions, and creams.
- It is also used as a natural remedy for sunburns and insect bites.
- Aloe Vera juice is consumed for digestive health.

Cultivation Tips
- Aloe Vera thrives in well-drained soil and should be kept in a sunny spot.
- It requires minimal watering and should not be overwatered, as this can lead to root rot.

Precautions
- Although generally safe, Aloe Vera gel should be used cautiously on sensitive skin, as it may cause irritation in some people.
